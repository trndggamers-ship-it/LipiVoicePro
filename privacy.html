Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 10, 2026

Welcome to LipiVoice Pro. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your data when you use our desktop software.

1. Data Collection & Offline Privacy

LipiVoice Pro is a 100% offline software. Unlike cloud-based tools, we do not upload your audio, video, or transcripts to any server. All processing happens locally on your computer's CPU/GPU.

We do not collect, store, or share your personal files.

2. Payment Information

We do not process payments directly. All transactions are handled securely by Gumroad. We do not have access to your credit card information.

3. Analytics

We use Google Analytics on our website (landing page) simply to understand visitor traffic. This does not track your usage inside the offline software.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via our Developer Contact Page.
