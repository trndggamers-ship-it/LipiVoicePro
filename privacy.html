Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

At LipiVoice Pro, accessible from our website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors and users.

1. Offline Processing

LipiVoice Pro is designed to function 100% offline. When you transcribe audio or video files, all processing happens locally on your computer's CPU/GPU. No audio data is ever sent to any cloud server or third party.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data, usage logs, or file contents from the software. Your files remain private on your machine.

3. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other websites (e.g., GitHub, Buy Me a Coffee). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.
